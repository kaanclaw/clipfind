How it works

Three steps.
Any footage.

No timecodes. No manual scrubbing. Just describe what you need.

📤
Upload your footage
Upload any MP4. Add optional context — "Dashcam from I-280, Jan 2024" — to improve accuracy. Indexing takes ~8 minutes per hour of footage.
🔍
Describe what you need
Type anything: "the moment of impact", "person entering from the left", "sharp turn on the highway". Gemini Vision AI understands visual context.
✂️
Get your clip
Results come ranked by confidence with exact timestamps. Preview inline. Export the trimmed MP4 in one click — ready for court, insurance, or editing.
Features

Built for real work

Every feature designed around how investigators, editors, and teams actually use footage.

⚡
Sub-second search
Once indexed, every search completes in under a second — regardless of how many hours of footage you have. Powered by ChromaDB vector search.
0.4saverage query time
🧠
Understands visual context
Finds moments based on what's happening visually — not just audio or captions. Works on silent footage.
📁
Add case context
Describe your footage when you upload. Better context = better results. Designed for professional evidence workflows.
✂️
One-click clip export
Every result comes with a trimmed MP4 clip. Preview inline. Download with one click. Chain-of-custody safe.
🔒
Your footage stays yours
No sharing, no training on your data. Files stay in your account. Self-host option available for enterprise.
📊
Confidence scoring
Every result comes with a similarity score so you know how confident the match is. Low-confidence results are flagged.
